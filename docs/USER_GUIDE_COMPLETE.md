## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit [yourdomain.com](https://yourdomain.com) or download our mobile app
2. Click **Sign Up** or **Create Account**
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Fill in your personal details:
   - First and last name
   - Phone number (optional but recommended for booking notifications)
5. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
6. You're ready to explore!

### Password Requirements

For your security, passwords must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include at least one uppercase letter
- Include at least one lowercase letter
- Include at least one number
- Include at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

### Logging In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. If you have **Two-Factor Authentication** enabled, enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
4. Click **Log In**

### Forgot Password?

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (valid for 1 hour)
4. Create a new password

## Making Bookings

### Chalet Bookings

Our resort offers beautiful chalets for overnight stays. Here's how to book:

#### Step 1: Search Available Chalets

1. Go to **Chalets** in the main menu
2. Select your desired dates:
   - **Check-in date**: When you'll arrive
   - **Check-out date**: When you'll leave
3. Enter the number of guests
4. Click **Search**

#### Step 2: Choose Your Chalet

Browse available chalets and view:
- Photos and virtual tours
- Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
- Maximum guest capacity
- Amenities (WiFi, pool access, kitchen, etc.)
- Price per night

Click on a chalet for more details or **Book Now** to proceed.

#### Step 3: Complete Your Booking

1. Review your booking details:
   - Dates and duration
   - Number of guests
   - Total price (including any seasonal adjustments)
2. Add any special requests (early check-in, specific needs, etc.)
3. Enter guest details if booking for someone else
4. Proceed to payment

#### Step 4: Payment

1. Enter your card details (secured by Stripe)
2. Review the total amount
3. Click **Confirm Payment**
4. You'll receive a confirmation email with your booking details

### Understanding Pricing

Our pricing varies based on:
- **Weekday vs Weekend**: Weekend nights may have different rates
- **Seasonal Adjustments**: Peak seasons (summer, holidays) may have higher rates
- **Length of Stay**: Longer stays may qualify for discounts

### Modifying Your Booking

Need to change your dates?

1. Go to **My Bookings**
2. Find your booking and click **Modify**
3. Select new dates
4. If available, you'll see any price difference
5. Confirm the changes and pay any additional amount (or receive credit)

### Cancelling Your Booking

Our cancellation policy:
- **More than 7 days before check-in**: Full refund
- **3-7 days before check-in**: 75% refund
- **1-3 days before check-in**: 50% refund
- **Less than 24 hours**: No refund (credit may be available)

To cancel:
1. Go to **My Bookings**
2. Find your booking and click **Cancel**
3. Provide a reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Refund will be processed within 5-10 business days

### Booking Credits

If you cancel and receive credits instead of a refund:
- Credits never expire
- Can be applied to any future booking
- Credits are automatically suggested at checkout

## Pool Access

### Day Passes

Purchase pool access for the day:

1. Go to **Pool** in the main menu
2. Select your visit date
3. Choose a time slot:
   - **Morning** (9:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
   - **Afternoon** (2:00 PM - 6:00 PM)
   - **Full Day** (9:00 AM - 6:00 PM)
4. Enter number of adults and children
5. Complete payment

### Your Pool Ticket

After purchase, you'll receive:
- A **QR code** ticket in your email
- The same QR code in the app under **My Tickets**

At the pool:
1. Show your QR code at the entrance
2. Staff will scan it to validate your entry
3. Enjoy the pool!

### Pool Memberships

For frequent visitors, consider a membership:

#### Membership Types

| Type | Description | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Individual** | Single person | Unlimited access, locker included |
| **Family** | Up to 4 members | Unlimited access, 2 guest passes/month |
| **Corporate** | Company account | Up to 10 members, dedicated area booking |
| **VIP** | Premium access | All benefits + priority lanes, spa access |

#### Signing Up for Membership

1. Go to **Pool** → **Memberships**
2. Select your plan
3. Choose billing cycle (monthly or annual)
4. For Family/Corporate: Add member details
5. Complete payment setup

#### Managing Your Membership

- View membership status under **Account** → **Memberships**
- Add or remove family members
- Use guest passes
- Pause or cancel anytime

## Restaurant Services

### Making a Reservation

1. Go to **Restaurant** in the main menu
2. Click **Make Reservation**
3. Select:
   - Date and time
   - Number of guests
   - Any preferences (window seat, high chair, etc.)
4. Enter guest name and phone
5. Confirm your reservation

You'll receive a confirmation with a reservation code.

### Viewing the Menu

Browse our menu before you visit:
1. Go to **Restaurant** → **Menu**
2. Filter by category (Appetizers, Mains, Desserts, etc.)
3. View allergen information and dietary options
4. Check today's specials

### Your Reservation

On the day of your reservation:
1. Arrive 10 minutes before your time
2. Give your name or reservation code to the host
3. You'll be seated at your assigned table

### Modifying/Cancelling Reservations

1. Go to **My Reservations**
2. Find your reservation
3. Click **Modify** to change details or **Cancel** to cancel
4. Please cancel at least 2 hours before your reservation time

## Account Management

### Your Profile

Update your personal information:
1. Click your avatar/name in the top right
2. Go to **Account Settings**
3. Update:
   - Name and contact details
   - Profile picture
   - Communication preferences

### Security Settings

#### Change Password

1. Go to **Account** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter and confirm new password
5. Save changes

#### Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

For enhanced security:
1. Go to **Account** → **Security**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

#### Active Sessions

View and manage your login sessions:
1. Go to **Account** → **Security** → **Active Sessions**
2. See all devices where you're logged in
3. Click **End Session** to log out from other devices

### Notification Preferences

Control what notifications you receive:
1. Go to **Account** → **Notifications**
2. Toggle preferences:
   - **Email notifications**: Booking confirmations, reminders
   - **Push notifications**: Real-time updates on mobile
   - **Marketing**: Special offers and promotions

### Privacy & Data

Under GDPR and privacy regulations:
- **Download your data**: Get a copy of all your data
- **Delete account**: Remove all your data permanently
- **Cookie preferences**: Manage tracking preferences

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Bookings

**Q: How far in advance can I book a chalet?**
A: You can book up to 12 months in advance.

**Q: Can I book multiple chalets at once?**
A: Yes, you can make multiple bookings. For group reservations (5+ chalets), contact our reservations team for special rates.

**Q: What time is check-in/check-out?**
A: Standard check-in is 3:00 PM, check-out is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request (subject to availability and fees).

**Q: Do chalets include pool access?**
A: Yes! All chalet guests receive complimentary pool access during their stay.

### Pool

**Q: Can children swim without adults?**
A: Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

**Q: What if the pool is closed due to weather?**
A: Full refunds or credits are provided for weather-related closures.

**Q: Can I transfer my membership to someone else?**
A: Individual memberships are non-transferable. Family memberships can update member details.

### Payments

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) through Stripe's secure payment system.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Absolutely. We use Stripe for all payments - we never store your full card details on our servers.

**Q: How long do refunds take?**
A: Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank.

### Account

**Q: I forgot my 2FA device. How do I log in?**
A: Use one of your backup codes. If you've lost those too, contact support with ID verification.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Account → Privacy → Delete Account. Note: This action is permanent and will cancel any active bookings.
